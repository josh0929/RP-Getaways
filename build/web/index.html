Due to the effects the sun has made on the water as he approached it, on the North Coast of the Island (Dominican Republic), Puerto Plata or "Port Silver" was given it's name by Christopher Columbus. Walking through the town of Puerto Plata you will experience and enjoy the everyday life of the locals with their colorful roadside shops offering anything from beautiful Amber and Larimar jewelry to local traditional souvenirs. Take the opportunity to sit in the park, sip from a fresh cocunut, visit one of the historic sites and bask in the warm hospitable nature of the Puertoplatenos making this a truly unforgettable experience.

The Dominican Republic is well known for it's beautiful beaches, clear ocean waters and sunny blue skies and Punta Cana is the culmination of all that perfect about the island. The area is best known for it's long streches of white sandy beaches and turquoise caribbean waters.
